About Us

Welcome to Image Sci

Image Sci (One stop processing of scientific research images) is a powerful online tool designed specifically for researchers, scientists, and academics. Our mission is to simplify the process of creating professional scientific image collages for publications, presentations, and research documentation.

Our Features

  • AI-Powered Analysis: Smart image arrangement using advanced AI technology
  • Automatic Format Conversion: Convert images to 600 DPI JPEG for publication quality
  • Customizable Layouts: Flexible column and size options for your needs
  • Figure Legend Generation: Auto-generate professional figure legends for top-tier journals
  • Scientific Image Support: Optimized for scientific figures including UMAP, flow cytometry, microscopy, and more
